ALHAJI SHEHU KATCHA

Full Biography: Alhaji Shehu Katcha was born in 1930 and he served as the secretary to the State Government(SMG) and Head of service(HOS) for a period. He held his position from August 1981 to 1983. He also worked at the Nigerian Railway Corporation(NRC) as a General Manager. He passed away in 2004 August 14, leaving a lasting legacy.
